## 📖 Overview

<a href="#on1Effects">ON1 Effects</a> is a dedicated photo effects and creative enhancement application — providing a deep library of professional-grade effects, filters, and presets that transform photographs with the cinematic, creative, and stylized looks that social media, commercial photography, and artistic photography require.

The <a href="#on1Effects">non-destructive filter stack</a> applies any combination of effects as layers that stack on top of the original image without permanently altering it — adding a cinematic color grade, a diffusion glow, a sun flare, and a grain texture as separate layers, each adjustable individually, each masked to specific image regions, and each removable without affecting the others. This layer-based effects approach enables complex multi-effect looks that remain fully editable throughout the creative process. The <a href="#on1Effects">preset library</a> provides hundreds of professionally designed starting points across categories — cinematic looks, vintage film emulations, landscape enhancement, portrait styles, black and white conversions, and creative artistic effects. Presets apply in a single click and serve as starting points for further customization rather than fixed outcomes, enabling rapid exploration of different creative directions for the same image.

The <a href="#on1Effects">Sky Swap AI tool</a> detects and replaces the sky in outdoor photographs with selected sky images from a built-in library — automatically adjusting the foreground lighting to match the new sky's conditions and blending the replacement convincingly. <a href="#on1Effects">Dynamic Contrast</a> adds punch and definition to images using local contrast enhancement that increases perceived sharpness and detail without the halo artifacts that conventional sharpening produces — making landscapes, architecture, and nature photography appear more vivid and defined without the over-processed look.

<a href="#on1Effects">Portrait retouching tools</a> include skin smoothing, blemish removal, eye enhancement, and facial detail refinement — the core portrait enhancement operations available as part of the effects workflow rather than requiring a separate retouching application. <a href="#on1Effects">Glow and diffusion effects</a> add the soft, ethereal quality associated with portrait photography, wedding photography, and romantic imagery — softening highlights, adding a luminous quality to skin tones, and creating the dreamlike atmosphere that diffusion filters on camera lenses traditionally provided.

<a href="#on1Effects">Sun flare and light effects</a> add realistic lens flares, light rays, bokeh overlays, and natural light effects to photographs — integrating convincingly with the existing image lighting rather than appearing as obvious overlay graphics. <a href="#on1Effects">Lightroom and Photoshop plugin</a> runs ON1 Effects from within the existing editing workflow — applying effects to photos from Lightroom and Photoshop without restructuring the creative process around a standalone application.
